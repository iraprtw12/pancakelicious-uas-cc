Welcome to Pancakelicious

Delivering great food for more than 18 years!

Our Menu Book a Table

Pancakelicious is a haven for pancake lovers! We are dedicated to serving the softest, fluffiest and tastiest pancakes in Cibinong. More than just ordinary pancakes, we offer a variety of unique and appetizing flavors and toppings. With high-quality ingredients and our secret recipe, every bite of our pancakes is an unforgettable experience. At Pancakelicious, we believe that happiness is in every drop of syrup and every bite of soft dough. We provide a comfortable and friendly atmosphere for you to relax and enjoy a meal with friends and family.

WHY US

Why Choose Our Restaurant

We use fresh, high-quality ingredients to ensure our pancakes are always delicious and flavorful.

We have a secret recipe that produces perfect pancakes soft, fluffy and delicious.

We offer a variety of flavors and toppings to satisfy all tastes. From classic toppings like blueberries and maple syrup to unique creations that will blow your mind, we have something for everyone.
